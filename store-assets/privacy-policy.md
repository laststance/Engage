# Privacy Policy - Engage App

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Overview

Engage ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application Engage (the "App").

**The short version: We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.**

## Information We Don't Collect

### Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information including but not limited to:

- Names, email addresses, or contact information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports
- Performance metrics

### App Usage Data

We do not collect any data about how you use the app, including:

- Which features you use
- How often you use the app
- What tasks you create or complete
- Your journal entries
- Your habit tracking data

## How Your Data is Stored

### Local Storage Only

All your data is stored locally on your device using:

- SQLite database for structured data (tasks, completions, journal entries)
- Device storage for app preferences
- No cloud storage or remote servers

### Data Types Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device:

- Custom tasks and categories you create
- Task completion records
- Daily journal entries
- App settings and preferences
- Statistics and progress data

## Data Security

### Device Security

Your data security depends on your device's security:

- Data is stored in the app's private storage area
- Access is controlled by your device's operating system
- We recommend using device lock screens and encryption

### No Network Transmission

Since we don't collect any data, there is no risk of data transmission or interception.

## Third-Party Services

### No Third-Party Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics services such as:

- Google Analytics
- Firebase Analytics
- Crashlytics
- Any other tracking or analytics services

### No Advertising

The app contains no advertisements and does not use any advertising networks.

### System Services Only

The app only uses standard system services provided by iOS and Android:

- Local notifications (with your permission)
- Local file storage
- Device calendar integration (if implemented)

## Permissions

### Notification Permission

The app may request permission to send local notifications to remind you about your daily habits. These notifications:

- Are generated locally on your device
- Do not send any data to external servers
- Can be disabled at any time in your device settings

### No Other Permissions

The app does not request any other permissions such as:

- Camera or photo access
- Location services
- Contacts access
- Microphone access
- Network access (beyond basic system requirements)

## Data Export and Backup

### Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

- Export your data at any time through the app's backup feature
- Data is exported in standard formats (JSON, CSV)
- You can manually backup your data to your preferred storage

### No Automatic Backups

We do not automatically backup your data to any cloud service. Any backups are initiated and controlled by you.

## Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for users of all ages and does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Since we don't collect any data, changes will typically be minor clarifications or updates to reflect new features.

## Data Deletion

### Uninstalling the App

When you uninstall the app, all your data is automatically deleted from your device as it's stored in the app's private storage area.

### Manual Data Deletion

You can delete specific data within the app:

- Delete individual tasks or journal entries
- Clear completion history
- Reset all data through app settings

## Technical Details

### Data Storage Location

- **iOS:** App's Documents directory in the app sandbox
- **Android:** App's private internal storage
- **Database:** SQLite database files
- **Preferences:** Platform-specific preference storage

### Data Format

- Structured data: SQLite database
- Settings: JSON format
- Exports: JSON and CSV formats

### Encryption

- Data is stored in plain text locally
- Device-level encryption (if enabled) provides additional security
- No network encryption needed as no data is transmitted

## Open Source

The app's privacy-focused approach is reflected in our development practices:

- Minimal dependencies to reduce privacy risks
- No analytics or tracking libraries
- Local-first architecture
- Transparent data handling

**Summary:** Engage is designed with privacy as a core principle. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. Your habit tracking data, journal entries, and app usage remain completely private and under your control.
